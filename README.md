# Smart Enter 🧠⌨️
**Reclaim the Carriage Return. Stop Accidental AI Prompts.**

`Smart Enter` is a privacy-first Chrome extension that brings the intentionality of the typewriter to the era of LLMs. It fixes the most frustrating UX flaw in modern AI interfaces: the "hair-trigger" Enter key.

---

## 📖 The Manifesto
The Enter key was originally a **carriage return**—a mechanical lever designed to move to the next line. Today’s chat apps have hijacked it, turning it into a "Send" button that fires off incomplete thoughts, destroys your context window, and wastes precious message limits.

Worse, every accidental send wastes electricity and evaporates water in data center cooling towers. **Smart Enter** returns the power to you.

## ✨ Features

* **The "Oops" Detector (Smart Mode):** Keep your muscle memory. Use Enter to send, but with a safety net. If you hit Enter but immediately keep typing, the extension catches the mistake, cancels the send, and converts it into a new line.
* **Absolute Control (Strict Mode):** Strip the Enter key of its sending power entirely. It becomes a pure carriage return. Map your "Send" command to a deliberate custom hotkey (like `Ctrl + Space` or `F12`).
* **Sustainability:** Stop the waste. Catching typos before they hit the API saves actual energy and water.
* **100% Privacy:** No tracking, no logging, no telemetry. Your keystrokes never leave your machine.
* **Native Support:** Works seamlessly on **ChatGPT** and **Google Gemini**.

## 🛠 Installation

Since this is an indie project, you can install it manually in seconds:

1.  **Clone or Download:** Clone this repo or download the `.zip` from the releases and extract it.
2.  **Open Extensions:** Open Google Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3.  **Enable Developer Mode:** Toggle the **Developer mode** switch in the top-right corner.
4.  **Load Unpacked:** Click the **Load unpacked** button and select the folder where you extracted the files.
5.  **Configure:** Click the **Smart Enter** icon in your toolbar to open the settings and test the "Oops" detector in the live playground.
